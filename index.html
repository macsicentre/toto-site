The Nature of High-Traffic Surges

Sporting events generate bursts of online activity. Whether it's a game-winning goal or controversial VAR decision, fans rush to news platforms for instant updates. These unpredictable traffic spikes require:

  • Flexible server capacity
  • Minimal latency
  • Efficient content delivery

If not handled properly, slow-loading pages and downtime can drive users away, harm brand credibility, and negatively impact SEO rankings.

To minimize server strain and enhance user experience, macsi-centre implements:
